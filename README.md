# MovieX (70k+ downloads)

MovieX is a Movies and Tv Explorer developed using <h4>The Movie Db api</h4>


<h2># Features</h2></br>

<ul>
<li>Explore all the latest movies and tv series and many other</li>
<li>Mark any movie or tv show to your watchlist (with login or without login)</li>
<li>Mark any movie or tv show to your Favorites(With login)</li>
<li>Login using TheMovieDb account</li>
<li>Track your tv episodes by marking them what you have already watched</li>
<li>Set any image as wallpaper of your phone directly</li>
<li>Explore details about your favorite celebs</li>
<li>Search for any movie/tv show/celebs</li>
<li>Rate any movie and tv show (with login)</li>
<li>see all reviews about movies</li>

</ul>
